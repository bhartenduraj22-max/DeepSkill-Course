# Analysis of Algorithms
Analysis of Algorithm tells us how well an algorithm performs. In analysis of algorithms we consider two parameters -Time and Space. Time refers to how long an algorithm takes to execute and Space refers to how much memory an algorithm requires during execution. Knowing DSA lets you design efficient algorithms that are fast, structured and solve the problem optimally. Time Complexity and Space Complexity are used to find and compare efficiencies of various methods. Asymptotic Notations are mathematical expressions that define the efficiency of an algorithm as the input tends to a specific value. Algorithms can be solved by calculating their efficiency under three cases such as Best Case, Average Case and Worst Case. Any algorithm, recursive or iterative can be analysed for finding its time complexity.
